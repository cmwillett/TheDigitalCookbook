# Crockpot Mac N Cheese

### This is for the kiddos!  Okay, we eat it too...

#### Ingredients
- 1 stick butter
- cooked macaroni
- 2 eggs beaten
- 2 cups milk
- 3 cups shredded cheddar cheese
- 1 cup cubed velveeta cheese

#### Directions
1. Dump it all in the slow cooker
2. Cook on high for 3 hours
