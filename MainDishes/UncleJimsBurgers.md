# Uncle Jim's Burgers

#### Ingredients:
- 2/3 cup ketchup
- 2 tbls worcestershire sauce
- 1/2 medium onion, grated
- 1 tbls pepper
- 3 lbs ground chuck

#### Directions:
1. Mix all ingredients, except the beef, in a mixing bowl
2. Mix with ground chuck and form patties to grill
